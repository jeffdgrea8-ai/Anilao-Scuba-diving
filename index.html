About Jefferson

KNOWN AS JEFF THE GREAT

Jefferson Zapata's story starts where every great diver's does — at the water's edge. He took his first breath underwater at the beach of Sabang, Puerto Galera, and never really came back up. That single dive set the course for the next two decades of his life.

His colleagues call him "Jeff the Great" — a nickname earned not just for logging over 10,000 dives, but for the rare ability to read a student's fear and turn it into confidence. After 12 years as a Divemaster, he earned his PADI and SDI Instructor ratings, bringing that depth of experience into every course he runs.

Now based in Anilao, Batangas — one of the Philippines' richest dive destinations — Jeff teaches across 100 mastered sites, from beginner reefs to technical walls, backed by certifications in both PADI and SDI systems.

FIND YOUR COURSE

Every course is taught in small groups — a maximum of four students — so you get genuine attention, not crowd management.

Beginner

Discover Scuba

A single afternoon in the water. No certification required — just curiosity and a willingness to breathe underwater for the first time.

Half day Pool + shallow reef No prerequisites
Entry Certification

Open Water Diver

The full PADI Open Water certification. Four classroom sessions, five confined dives, and four open-water dives — all at your pace.

3–4 days Certifies to 18m Ages 10+
Intermediate

Advanced Open Water

Five adventure dives including deep and navigation. Designed to build confidence in diverse conditions and unlock dive sites most instructors skip.

2 days To 30m depth OW required
Safety

Rescue Diver

The course most divers call their most challenging — and their most rewarding. Learn to manage and prevent dive emergencies before they escalate.

2–3 days EFR required AOW recommended
Specialty

Night Diver

The reef transforms after dark. This specialty shows you how to navigate and read marine behavior in low visibility — an entirely different dive.

1 evening 3 night dives OW required
Professional

Divemaster

The first professional PADI rating. A mentored program conducted over several weeks — for those who want to lead dives and assist with instruction.

6–10 weeks 60 logged dives Rescue required
